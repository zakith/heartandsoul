Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend calling ahead for parties of 6 or more, especially on weekends.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We cater events of all sizes. Contact us for a custom quote and menu options.

Are there vegetarian options available?

Yes, we offer several vegetarian sides and can accommodate dietary restrictions. Please let us know your needs.

Do you offer takeout and delivery?

Yes! We offer takeout for all menu items. Delivery is available through third-party services.
